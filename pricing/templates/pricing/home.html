How It Works

1

Seasonal Adjustment

Base Rate × Season Index

Example: $65 × 1.3 = $84.50

2

Add Meals

+ (Meal Supplement × 2 pax)

Example: $84.50 + ($6 × 2) = $96.50

3

Channel Discount

× (1 - Discount%)

Example: $96.50 × 0.85 = $82.03
