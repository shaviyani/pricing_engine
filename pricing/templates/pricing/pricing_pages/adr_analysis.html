How ADR is Calculated

Simple Average: Equal weight to all room types, rate plans, channels, and modifiers

OTA Heavy: 70% of bookings via OTA channels, 30% direct

Direct Heavy: 60% direct bookings (higher discounts, lower commission)

Budget Mix: More guests choosing room only and B&B options

Premium Mix: More guests opting for half board and full board

RevPAR: ADR multiplied by assumed occupancy rate (60%, 75%, or 90%)
